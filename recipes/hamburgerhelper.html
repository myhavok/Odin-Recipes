Homemade Hamburger Helper


Description

Copycat cheeseburger macaroni version of a Hamburger Helper

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef 93% lean
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 15 oz. tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
  • 5 C. beef broth
  • 16 oz. medium shell noodles
  • 1 & 1/2 C. sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  • 1 C. mild cheddar cheese shredded
  • 1/3 C. mayonnaise
  • 1/4 C. ketchup
  • 1 Tbsp. mustard
  • 1/2 C. pickle slices diced
  • 1/4 C. green onions optional

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown ground beef and garlic powder. Drain.
  2. Leaving heat on medium, stir in tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, & pepper.
  3. Add beef broth and noodles. Stir well. Bring to a boil.
  4. Lower heat and simmer, covered, for 10-15 minutes until noodles are done and liquid is mostly absorbed. Stir every few minutes throughout cooking process.
  5. Add sharp cheddar and mild cheddar. Stir until melted. Remove from heat.
  6. Stir in mayonnaise, ketcup, mustard, and pickles.
  7. Serve garnished with green onions, if desired.
